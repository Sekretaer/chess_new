About Us
We're a little, traditional chess club with ca. 50 members and are welcome to new members! In our club you are able to learn chess, to play for fun or to participate in team matches.
Our trainer team consists of two top players (2100 Elo) who who are happy to teach interested players who want to play better and to show the interesting side of chess.
Last, but not least, we have an internal tournament every month and participate in a lot of external Tournaments where you can test your skills which you've gained in the lessons.
